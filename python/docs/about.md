# About

## Developer

pilotr is developed by **[Pablo Bernabeu](https://pablobernabeu.github.io)**, a researcher at
the Department of Education, University of Oxford. He works across cognitive psychology,
linguistics, and research methods, with particular interests in statistical methodology and
power analysis, open science, and reproducibility. pilotr continues that line of work, building
on his earlier *Experimental-data-simulation* Shiny app (Bernabeu & Lynott, 2024).

- Website: <https://pablobernabeu.github.io>
- ORCID: [0000-0003-1083-2460](https://orcid.org/0000-0003-1083-2460)
- GitHub: [@pablobernabeu](https://github.com/pablobernabeu)

## Citing pilotr

If you use pilotr in published work, please cite it:

> Bernabeu, P. (2026). *pilotr: Simulate experimental and behavioral data from a portable
> design specification* (Version 0.1.0) [Computer software].
> <https://github.com/pablobernabeu/pilotr>

BibTeX:

```bibtex
@software{bernabeu2026pilotr,
  author  = {Pablo Bernabeu},
  title   = {{pilotr}: Simulate Experimental and Behavioral Data from a Portable Design Specification},
  year    = {2026},
  version = {0.1.0},
  url     = {https://github.com/pablobernabeu/pilotr}
}
```

In R, `citation("pilotr")` returns the same reference.
